What the words mean
  • Reach. Number of people who saw the reel at all. Bigger reach = IG pushed it.
  • Save rate. % of people who hit Save. The strongest signal a reel actually resonated, way more meaningful than likes.
  • Completion rate. Average % of the reel watched. Not "watched to the end", average across all viewers.
  • Median (vs average). The middle reel: half scored higher, half lower. Used instead of average so one breakout doesn't distort the picture.
  • Rolling baseline. The median across every reel ever, qualified at 500+ reach. Anything 2x baseline = a hit. This week's baseline is 1.17%.
