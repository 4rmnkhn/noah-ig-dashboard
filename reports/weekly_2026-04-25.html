What the words mean

If you're skimming and the jargon is annoying

  • ReachThe number of people who saw the reel at all. Bigger reach = the IG algorithm pushed it.
  • Save rateThe % of people who hit Save. The strongest signal that a reel actually resonated — way more meaningful than likes.
  • Completion rateThe % of the reel watched on average. Not "watched to the end" — average across all viewers.
  • Median (vs average)The middle reel — half scored higher, half lower. Used instead of average so one breakout doesn't distort the picture.
  • Rolling baselineThe median across every reel ever, qualified at 500+ reach. Anything 2x baseline = a hit.
  • Format / PillarWhat kind of reel it is — Green-Screen, Talking-Head, Podcast clip, etc. Set in the Notion tracker.
