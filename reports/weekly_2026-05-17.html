✕ 4 patterns that bled save rate this week. Don't repeat them next week.

  • "Most underrated" / "most overlooked" / "the main factor" tease hooks.

    Saved at 0.46% on 220 reach. The "…" tells the viewer the answer is hidden. The viewer cannot save what they cannot read. Drop the ellipsis and name the first of the three things in the hook itself. "Start split testing a higher price" is the actual body opener: that should have been the hook.

    The 0-30s band still saves at 0.26% median rolling: the worst band by a wide margin. The 46-60s band is now the clear rolling leader at 1.97% median, well above 90s+ at 1.23% and 61-90s at 1.17%. Default new scripts to 46-60s. Push to 61-90s only when the topic genuinely needs the runway.

What the words mean
  • Reach. Number of people who saw the reel at all. Bigger reach means IG pushed it.
  • Save rate. % of people who hit Save. The strongest signal a reel actually resonated: way more meaningful than likes.
  • Completion rate. Average % of the reel watched. Not "watched to the end": average across all viewers.
  • Median (vs average). The middle reel: half scored higher, half lower. Used instead of average so one breakout doesn't distort the picture.
  • Rolling baseline. The median across every reel ever, qualified at 500+ reach. Anything 2x baseline is a hit.
