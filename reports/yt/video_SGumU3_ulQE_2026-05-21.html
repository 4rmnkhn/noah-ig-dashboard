How to read this report

Discovery Score
How well this video pulls new viewers via YouTube search + suggested traffic. High = broad topic, curiosity-baited title, no insider jargon, accessible to a cold viewer.
Mechanism Score
How tactical this video is. High = single named system demonstrated end-to-end with specific tools/numbers + replicable by viewer.
Offer Proximity Score
How close this video is to triggering the $4-5K coaching offer purchase. High = directly demonstrates the buying outcome (live deal closes, case studies, "this is what we do together").
Primary Stage
Derived from the dominant dimension. TOF = top-of-funnel acquisition (Discovery wins). MOF = middle-funnel pre-sell (Mechanism wins). BOF = bottom-funnel trust-build (Offer Proximity wins).
